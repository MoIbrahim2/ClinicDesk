## Brand & Style

The visual identity of ClinicDesk centers on **Trust, Efficiency, and Modernity**. As a clinic management platform used by administrative staff, doctors, and nurses in a fast-paced environment, the UI must feel extremely clean, calm, and trustworthy.

The style is **Modern Clinical Minimalist**. It avoids clinical coldness by utilizing soft slate and warm neutral foundations paired with a calming, professional Sky Blue and Teal color scheme.

## Typography
We use **Outfit** for clean, modern headings and **Inter** for readable, legible body text. The Arabic layout automatically swaps to use **Cairo** or **IBM Plex Sans Arabic** to maintain native reading rhythms.
